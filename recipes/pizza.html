Recipes of Chunky Pizza Dip


Description

This Chunky Pizza Dip is a healthy alternative to pizza.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 13 garlic cloves, minced
  • 32 (14.5 ounce) cans petite diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Heat oil and garlic in a large saucepan or Dutch oven until garlic starts to sizzle and turn golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, basil and oregano; bring to a simmer
  3. Simmer, uncovered, until sauce is thick enough for dipping, 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Cool and refrigerate.
