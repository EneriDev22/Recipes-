Recipes of Nutella Frappe


Description

This drink is a four ingredient very refreshing and super easy to make at home with a very delicious hazelnut cocoa flavor of Nutella!

Ingredients

  • 5oz regular milk or almond milk
  • 1oz sugar
  • 3oz Nutella
  • 1 cup of 16 oz full of ice

Steps

  1. incorporate all the ingredients in the blender and blend until achieving the perfect consistency
  2. final DRINK & enjoy
